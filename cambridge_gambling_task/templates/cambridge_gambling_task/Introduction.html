在你面前有10个宝箱，其中一些是F类型的宝箱，另一些是J类型的宝箱。一枚金币隐藏在其中一个宝箱内。你的任务是猜测金币藏在哪个 类型 的宝箱内。游戏包含多个回合，在每个回合你都有一次猜测的机会。猜测时需要扣除你现有的积分作为成本。 (In front of you are 10 treasure chests, some of which are type F and others are type J. Guessing will deduct from your existing points as a cost.)

每 {{ round_interactions }} 个回合为一个阶段。在每个阶段开始时，你的积分将设置为 {{ init_money }}。你的目标是最大化每个阶段的积分，每个阶段的积分之和就是你在这场游戏中所获得的总积分。(Every {{ round_interactions }} rounds constitute a phase. At the start of each phase, your points will be set to {{ init_money }}. Your goal is to maximize the points in each phase, and the sum of the points from each phase is your total score in the game.)

每回合，你需要通过从现有积分中扣除一定的比例来下注选择宝箱的类型。可选的比例有： 5%、25%、50%、75%、95%。(Each round, you need to bet by deducting a certain percentage from your existing points to choose the type of chest. The available percentages are: 5%, 25%, 50%, 75%, 95%.)

如果金币藏在你猜测类型的 任意一个 宝箱内，你就赢了；否则，你就输了。(If the gold coin is hidden in any one of the chests of the type you guessed, you win; otherwise, you lose.)

如果你赢了，你会获得双倍下注的积分；如果你输了，你什么也得不到。积分将四舍五入为最接近的整数。(If you win, you will receive double the points of your bet; if you lose, you gain nothing. Points are rounded to the nearest whole number.)

一个重要的游戏设定是：金币的位置在每一回合都是完全随机的，和之前的位置没有任何联系。即每回合的金币位置是相互独立的事件。(An important game setting is: the position of the gold coin is completely random in each round and has no connection to previous locations. That is, each round's coin position is an independent event.)
